# Week 3 — Challenges

1. **[Challenge 1 — One-Command Spin-Up with Seed Data](challenge-01-one-command-spinup-with-seed-data.md)** — From a fresh clone, a single command brings up a four-service stack, runs migrations, seeds the database, and exits with every service `(healthy)` in under 60 seconds. (~3 hours)

Challenges are graded on the **final outcome** and the **journal that documents how you got there**. We want the dead ends, not just the polished result. Squash before submission only if you keep a separate `journal.md` with the timeline.

This week's challenge is the synthesis of Lectures 1 and 2 and Exercises 1–3: every concept you learned, applied to one deliverable that *just works* on someone else's machine.
