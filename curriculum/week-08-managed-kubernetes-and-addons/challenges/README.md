# Week 8 — Challenges

Two challenges, ~3 hours total. Challenges are harder than exercises; they may require pulling pieces together from multiple sources. The point is the diagnosis, not the solution.

| # | Title | Time | Skill |
|---|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | [Port from kind to Autopilot](./challenge-01-port-from-kind-to-autopilot.md) | 90 min | Portability, manifest editing |
| 2 | [Debug a stuck ArgoCD sync](./challenge-02-debug-a-stuck-argocd-sync.md) | 90 min | Diagnosis, reading events |

Both challenges expect you to write up the diagnostic process — not just the answer. The hiring market values engineers who can show how they think, not engineers who can show the bug fix in isolation.
