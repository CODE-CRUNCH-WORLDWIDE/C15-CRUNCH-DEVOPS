# Week 6 — Challenges

The challenge is the optional sharpening exercise of the week. It is not required for graduation from C15; it is required for the engineer you want to be at the end of C15.

| Challenge | Title | Time | Difficulty |
|-----------|-------|------|------------|
| [01](./challenge-01-rebuild-vs-patch.md) | Build a rebuild-vs-patch runbook for a real incident shape | 2-3 hours | Hard |

---

## How challenges differ from exercises

- **Exercises** are scripted. You follow the steps; the steps work; you understand what happened.
- **Challenges** are open-ended. The goal is given; the steps are not. You assemble the pieces from the lecture notes, the exercises, the provider docs, and your own taste.

Cost: $0.00. The challenge is a writing exercise plus a small reproduction lab on the `kind` clusters you already have up (or that you can re-create in under five minutes).

The challenge is graded on three things, in order of weight: **does the runbook work** (a teammate could follow it without you in the room), **does the decision rule survive contact with reality** (the four incident shapes in the challenge each push on a different edge of the rule), and **does the write-up explain the trade-offs** (the cost of rebuild vs the cost of patch in each shape).
